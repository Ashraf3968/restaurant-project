About Aurelio House

Built around hospitality that feels composed, warm, and exacting.

Every element, from the menu structure to the room lighting, is calibrated to help guests settle into an evening worth remembering.

Brand story

From intimate supper clubs to a landmark dining address.

Aurelio House began as a chef-led series of private dinners for guests who wanted restaurant-grade craft in a more intimate, thoughtful setting.

Today, the house is known for seasonal fire-led menus, sommelier pairings, and service shaped around quiet confidence rather than spectacle.
